Week 9

Vinyl Cutter Design

Butterfly SweatshirtOctober 28, 2020

Float like a Butterfly This weeks project was to use the vinyl cutter and make a design for a sweatshirt. The design had to be at least 15 characters and it had to have a grapic that was at least 4x4.

For my design i choose the Muhammed Ali quote "float like a butterfly, sting like a bee". I used a green sweatshirt and white t-shirt Vinyl. On the back of the hoodie the quote is at the top. On the sleeves there are white buuterflies and on the pocket there is also a bee hive to symbolize the bees.
